Meet your coach

I’m a certified personal trainer and yoga instructor. My passion lies in helping clients achieve their personal health goals while enjoying their well-being journey. I believe in dynamic exercises that blend physical and mental practices, emphasising the love for yoga classes and stretching. Let’s work together to create a balanced, sustainable fitness routine that brings joy and vitality to your life!

I have the knowledge to provide a variety of classes and have the skills and abilities to design safe and effective exercise programmes with over 5 years of experience. I currently work out of a private studio in Reading & also offer home/mobile/online classes.

I can't wait to welcome you to my new training program and help you achieve the perfect shape of you! I look forward to seeing you soon!

Get Started

When I first met yoga, I was open to trying it out. In the beginning, I practised at home after I decided to go to gym classes, where I attended yoga Hatha and Vinyasa. When I stepped on the yoga mat and started practising Surya Namaskar, it was a fantastic feeling, physically and mentally filled up me, and I was reborn. I find harmony in yoga!

My mission is to show you Yoga is, where you find the perfect balance between body and mind.
Focus on your breath and control your movements by your consciousness, and stillness as well as enhance athleticism, and fluidity in movement.
Learning how to listen to our body and feel it evolve.

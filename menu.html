Meet your coach

I’m a certified personal trainer and yoga instructor. My passion lies in helping clients achieve their personal health goals while enjoying their well-being journey. I believe in dynamic exercises that blend physical and mental practices, emphasising the love for yoga classes and stretching. Let’s work together to create a balanced, sustainable fitness routine that brings joy and vitality to your life! I have the knowledge to provide a variety of classes and have the skills and abilities to design safe and effective exercise programmes with over 5 years of experience. I currently work out of a private studio in Reading & also offer home/mobile/online classes.

Get Started

When I first met yoga, I was open to trying it out. In the beginning, I practised at home after I decided to go to gym classes, where I attended yoga Hatha and Vinyasa. When I stepped on the yoga mat and started practising Surya Namaskar, it was a fantastic feeling, physically and mentally filled up me, and I was reborn. I find harmony in yoga. My mission is to show you Yoga is, where you find the perfect balance between body and mind. Focus on your breath and control your movements by your consciousness, and stillness as well as enhance athleticism, and fluidity in movement. Learning how to listen to our body and feel it evolve. I'm lovely to show it to others, how can they use yoga Asanas to fill up the body with life force?

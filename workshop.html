What to Expect from the Backbend Workshop

This workshop is designed for anyone looking to deepen their understanding of backbends, whether you’re a beginner or want to refine your technique. We’ll focus on building a strong foundation, emphasizing safe alignment and mindful movement to protect your spine and joints.

Comprehensive Warm-Up and Preparation You’ll start with effective warm-up sequences that prepare your shoulders, spine, and hips for backbending. Expect dynamic stretches and mobility drills—such as cat-cow, glute bridges, and lunges—to ensure your body is ready for deeper work. Step-by-Step Progressions We’ll break down the mechanics of key backbending poses, from gentle options like cobra and bridge to more advanced shapes. You’ll learn how to engage the right muscles, especially the glutes, core, and back, to support your spine and avoid compression. Mobility and Strength Drills Through targeted exercises, you’ll improve flexibility in your shoulders and hip flexors and build strength in your posterior chain. Drills may include prone shoulder lifts, couch stretches, and thoracic spine mobility work—each chosen to help you safely progress toward deeper backbends. Breathwork and Body Awareness Breathing techniques will be integrated throughout the session to support healthy spinal movement, enhance focus, and help you relax into each pose. You’ll also develop greater awareness of your body’s limits and learn how to listen to its signals. Individual Guidance and Props With a small group setting, you’ll receive personalized feedback and hands-on adjustments as appropriate. Props such as blocks and straps will be used to make backbends accessible and comfortable for everyone, regardless of experience level

What You’ll Learn How to safely approach and progress backbends, from foundational to peak poses Techniques for improving spinal flexibility, shoulder mobility, and hip extension How to engage and strengthen supporting muscles for stability and injury prevention The role of breathwork in enhancing your practice and protecting your spine How to adapt poses for your unique body, including modifications for common injuries or limitations Ways to use props and self-adjustments to deepen your backbends with confidence

Benefits You’ll Experience Increased spinal flexibility and mobility Improved posture and alignment, especially beneficial for those who spend long hours sitting Release of tension in the back, chest, and shoulders Greater energy, focus, and a sense of balance in your practice and daily life Practical tools to continue progressing in your backbend journey safely

Whether you’re looking to master a full wheel, deepen your camel, or simply feel more open and energized, this workshop will give you the knowledge, skills, and confidence to enjoy backbends safely and effectively.

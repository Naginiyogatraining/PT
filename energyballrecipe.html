Energy Balls

No Bake!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dates
  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • ½ cup hazelnuts
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • ⅛ cup honey
  • ½ cup oat milk

Combine all ingredients into a food processor, then blend it to make a paste for the base of these balls. Create 1-inch balls from the mixture. Roll the balls in cocoa powder. Enjoy!

Dates are sweet fruits that may support heart, digestive, and brain health. It is a good source of potassium, magnesium, iron and antioxidants. The nuts are a rich source of many vital nutrients, boasting high levels of manganese, copper, and vitamin E.
